Orbital Drift

9 Holes · Par 24

How to Play

Mouse / Touch

  • Click (or tap) and drag backward to aim
  • The aim line shows your shot direction
  • Drag further for more power
  • Release to shoot

Gameplay

  • Get the ball in the hole in the fewest strokes
  • Water hazards add a 1-stroke penalty
  • Complete all 9 holes for your final score

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • D — Toggle debug mode
  • Esc — Pause / Resume
